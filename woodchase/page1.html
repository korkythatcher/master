Architectural Control Committee (ACC):
 
The Architectural Control Committee (ACC) was established to ensure our neighborhood restrictions are being followed to maximize our property values. Recently, we have noticed that some of our neighborhood restrictions are not being followed. For example, there are many signs being placed in yards advertising a contractor doing work on a property, school signs, children at play signs, etc. If we allow these signs to be displayed, then that opens us up to having to allow people to start displaying other signs, including political signs, which the Board does not want. So, please remove all signs in your yard as quickly as possible. The only permissible signs that can be displayed in Woodchase are "For Sale" and Security/Alarm Company signs.
 
Also, before you start your next outdoor project, please contact our Architectural Control Committee (ACC) for approval. The ACC must approve, before any work is performed, all landscaping, replacement roofing plans, replacing fencing, window replacements, painting, and most renovations plans. If you do not get the appropriate approval, you could face a large fine/penalty.
